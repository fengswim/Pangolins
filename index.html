什麼是穿山甲？ What is a Pangolin?

穿山甲是地球上唯一擁有「鱗片」的哺乳動物。牠們分布在非洲和亞洲，全世界共有八個品種。牠們就像森林裡的和平小士兵，身體覆蓋著由角質（跟我們的指甲成分一樣）組成的堅硬鱗片。

Pangolins are the only mammals on Earth with large keratin scales. There are eight species found in Africa and Asia. They are like peaceful forest soldiers, covered in tough scales made of keratin, the same material as our fingernails.

成長之旅 (從初生到成年) Growth Journey (From Birth to Adult)

🐣 初生幼崽 (Newborn Pup)

初生時鱗片是柔軟的，會騎在媽媽的尾巴上。

Born with soft scales and rides on mother's tail.

🌿 少年時期 (Juvenile)

鱗片逐漸變硬，開始學習挖洞和覓食。

Scales harden, starts learning to burrow and forage.

🛡️ 成年鎧甲精靈 (Adult)

擁有強大的鎧甲和長舌頭，是獨立的森林精靈。

Equipped with strong armor and long tongue, an independent forest sprite.

神奇的生物習性 Amazing Facts

🏰 🏰 挖洞大師 (Burrowing Expert)

牠們強壯的前爪能挖開地表，為森林翻鬆土壤。

Strong front claws burrow, churning soil for forest health.

🐜 🐜 森林清道夫 (Forest Scavenger)

一隻每年吃掉 7,000 萬隻白蟻，保護樹木不被破壞。

Eats 70 million termites yearly, protecting trees.

🌀 🌀 防禦姿態 (Defense Stance)

遇到危險時，縮成一個球，保護柔軟的肚子。

Rolls into a ball when threatened, protecting its soft belly.
