什麼是穿山甲？ What is a Pangolin?

穿山甲是地球上唯一擁有「鱗片」的哺乳動物。牠們就像森林裡的和平小士兵，身體覆蓋著由角質（跟我們的指甲成分一樣）組成的堅硬鱗片。

Pangolins are the only mammals on Earth with large keratin scales. They are like peaceful forest soldiers, covered in tough scales made of the same material as our fingernails.
