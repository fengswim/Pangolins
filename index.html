極危 (CR) Critically Endangered

中華穿山甲

Chinese Pangolin

分布於中國南方及東南亞，喜愛挖掘深洞。

Found in Southern China and SE Asia, loves deep burrowing.

易危 (VU) Vulnerable

大穿山甲

Giant Pangolin

穿山甲家族中體型最大的成員，主要生活在非洲。

The largest member of the family, living mainly in Africa.

💡 Did You Know?

穿山甲沒有牙齒！牠們透過吞食小砂石進入胃部（砂囊）來幫助磨碎螞蟻。

Pangolins have no teeth! They swallow small stones to help grind up insects in their stomachs.
