科學概述 Scientific Overview

穿山甲（學名：Pholidota），古稱鯪鯉，是哺乳綱真獸下綱的一個目。其特徵為全身覆蓋著重疊的角質鱗片，這些鱗片佔據其體重的 20%。牠們在演化上是一個非常古老的群體，主要分布於熱帶及亞熱帶地區。

Pangolins (Order: Pholidota) are mammals characterized by overlapping scales made of keratin, which account for about 20% of their body weight. They are an ancient evolutionary group found in tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

生理與解剖 Physiology & Anatomy

🛡️ 鱗片結構

穿山甲的鱗片由角質蛋白構成，會隨著成長不斷更新磨損。鱗片邊緣銳利，可用於切傷捕食者的嘴部。

👅 捕食機制

牠們沒有牙齒，但擁有一條長達 40 厘米的舌頭。舌頭的起點甚至連接在骨盆附近，附帶著黏性的唾液，用於捕捉螞蟻。

🐾 強力前爪

前肢中趾發達，長有強壯的利爪，能輕易刨開極為堅硬的蟻冢。

🛡️ Scale Structure

The scales are made of keratin and grow continuously. The sharp edges can slash at the muzzles of predators.

👅 Feeding Mechanism

They lack teeth but possess a tongue up to 40cm long. The tongue is anchored near the pelvis and coated with sticky saliva to capture insects.

🐾 Powerful Claws

Strong, curved claws on their front feet allow them to dig through concrete-like termite mounds.

保育現狀與威脅 Conservation Status

根據維基百科與 IUCN 的數據，穿山甲是目前**全球非法貿易量最高**的哺乳動物。由於棲息地喪失和非法捕殺，全部 8 個物種均被列入瀕危名單，其中 3 種為「極危」級別。

According to Wikipedia and IUCN data, pangolins are the **most trafficked mammals** in the world. Due to habitat loss and poaching, all 8 species are listed as threatened, with 3 species being "Critically Endangered."
